Welcome to Rolyo Films

Introduction

The name “Rolyo” represents the connection between past and present — the dedication to precision and quality that carried through from analog film to digital innovation. Every project is approached with the same careful planning, attention to detail, and passion that defined cinema’s golden age, but powered by today’s cutting-edge tools. Founded in 2024, Rolyo Films was born with a deep respect for the history of filmmaking, honoring craftsmanship and storytelling with heart while embracing modern technology.

About Us

Rolyo Films Production is a creative team dedicated to capturing powerful stories through photography and film. We believe in the art of storytelling, bringing emotions and memories to life with every project we produce.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To bring compelling stories to life with passion, creativity, and a commitment to excellence in every frame.


Vision: To produce timeless films that inspire, engage, and reflect the richness of human experiences.

Core Values

  • Passion for Storytelling
  • Craftsmanship
  • Innovation + Tradition
  • Collaboration
  • Excellence

Goal

Produce Award-Winning Films – Compete in local and international film festivals.

Expand Distribution – Partner with streaming platforms and cinema chains nationwide.

Talent Development – Launch workshops to train aspiring filmmakers and actors.

Build a Studio Facility – Establish a state-of-the-art production hub for local talents.

Promote Philippine Culture – Create films that highlight Filipino heritage and stories for global audiences.

Our Milestone

  • 2024 – Rolyo Films founded, inspired by the golden age of cinema.
  • Jan 2025 – Released Pilas, Lapis, a docu-drama highlighting women’s courage against discrimination and abuse.
  • Apr 2025 – Produced EverTrue, a short film for the Lenten season.
  • 2025 – Released a commercial project for brand promotion.
